'''Emma''' is a character in [[Four Leaf]].
== Initial Appearance ==
Emma makes her debut in ''[https://www.webtoons.com/en/fantasy/four-leaf/episode-22-a-place-to-hide/viewer?title_no=1454&amp;episode_no=22 Episode 22 - A Place To Hide]'' by crashing through a window in [[Cricket|Cricket's]] home while trying to get away from a gang of [[Red Hoods]]. It is here that she also comes across the main three protagonists of the story.

== Search For The Lady ==
In ''[https://www.webtoons.com/en/fantasy/four-leaf/episode-24-the-festival-of-roses/viewer?title_no=1454&amp;episode_no=24 Episode 24 - The Festival of Roses],'' [[Guadalupe Jara|Lupe]] and Emma briefly speak about the mountains, the known general location of The Lady. Emma tells her that she's also in search of [[The Lady]] for a wish, but what that wish could be is not yet known.
