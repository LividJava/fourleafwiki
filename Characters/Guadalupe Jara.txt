The protagonist of Four Leaf, Guadalupe Jara (preferring to go by Lupe) is a student who seems to have issues dreaming and calls herself Non-Magical.

== Initial Appearance ==
First appearing in Episode 1, she is seen falling from the sky, then waking up to her alarm, realizing she had remembered her dream, for the first time in her life in 16 years.

== Physical Appearance ==
Lupe in her school uniform, shown in Episode 1 (see infobox). Her school bottoms can be interchanged with grey pants and a grey skirt with a darker shade of leggings underneath. She has orange hair with brown eyes. Kept consistent throughout outfits, is Lupe's forest green shoes, which match with Lina's eyes when shown as her imaginary friend. Out of school, Lupe wears a black shirt and red pants. These pants have a green-yellow waistband and buttoned pockets which line the front. They're wide at the bottom which has magenta cuffs, a shade similar to the pockets. At the top, on the sides, is a bell design with 'X's.
