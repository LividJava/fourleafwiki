The Lady or as her name is later revealed to be Alice, is a supporting character.

Initial Appearance

The Lady is usually referred just by second-hand accounts since her existence is questioned by the majority of the population because of her status as being just a character from the fairy tale, Four Leaf (Private).

The Lady has been mentioned throughout the initial episodes as the ultimate goal of Lupe and Lina to wish Lupe back home.

== Wishes Granted ==

  • Liam Lambert, father of Alvar Lambert (Private), wished for a weapon that would allow him to face the witches, so The Lady gave him a stone that the hunters forged into The Dragonfly (Private).
  • Carolina (Private) wished for a friend which ended up sending Guadalupe Jara (Private) to Carolina's world.
