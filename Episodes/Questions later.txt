== Synopsis ==
Lupe starts dreaming for the first time and meets an unexpected friend.

== Plot ==
Lupe had come to terms with the fact that Lina was nothing more than her Imaginary friend, and thus she went to sleep, not expecting to dream. But after she fell asleep, she wakes up in a different world saved by none other than Lina. Lupe then follows Lina to the subway, on the way to Lina's house. On the subway, it is revealed that Lupe was Lina's imaginary friend.
