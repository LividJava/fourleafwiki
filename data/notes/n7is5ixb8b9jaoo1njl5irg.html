Initial Appearance

Emma makes her debut in Episode 22 by crashing through a window in Cricket's home while trying to get away from a gang of Red Hoods. It is here that she also comes across the main three protagonists of the story.

Search For The Lady

In Episode 24 Lupe and Emma briefly speak about the mountains, the known general location of The Lady. Emma tells her that she's also in search of The Lady for a wish, but what that wish could be is not yet known.
